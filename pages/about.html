WIE IS VISPET

Vispet is a young Art Direction and Graphic Design agency based in Bruges (Belgium), founded by Chiel Chielens. Vispet provide Art Direction, Graphic Design, Branding and Identity Development. With 3 years experience in a communication agency I developed a lot of experience in the printing world. I worked for clients such as Electrolux, Biennale interieur, Winsol, Voka,...

When i was young my passion for graphic design grew because of my dad that was also a graphic designer, now he is a screen printer. So when i was young i often did my homework at the graphic agency where my dad was working. There i first noticed the wonderfull smell of fresh offset printing work.

I cant walk in a city without collecting all sorts of printings materials or looking and judging logos and typography by myself.

Graphic design is my work and my live.

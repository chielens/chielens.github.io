WIE IS VISPET

Oh Snap Grfx is a young Art Direction, Graphic Design and Motion Studio based in Ghent (Belgium), founded by Wouter Lierman. With several years of experiences in advertising, interactive and visual communication, our approach is highly collaborative and focused on innovation and creativity. We love to work within the overlap of art and design! Oh Snap has brought an artistic slant to numerous projects for clients such as Knack, Vice, Komrad Invasion, TMF, Carlsberg, Jansport and many others. Established in 2012, Oh Snap executes tailored solutions in any medium to meet the communication needs of our clients.
